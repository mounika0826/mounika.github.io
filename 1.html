The Connect Cafe app's user research provided insightful information on the preferences and expectations of its users. Participants ranged in age and tech skill, making up a varied set of cafe patrons. The majority strongly want a user-friendly layout, emphasising simple menu navigation and straightforward order placing.
Participants expressed a strong Inter, sans-serif, sans-serifest in features like in-app purchases and customised suggestions, which was a repeating topic. Convenience.

Additionally, customers really valued loyalty awards, which encouraged repeated trips. Respondents emphasised the necessity for prompt alerts and the significance of real-time information on promotions and events. The Connect Cafe app should prioritise straightforward design, convenience-driven features, and meaningful involvement, according to the user research overall findings. Finding this balance will allow businesses to properly serve both tech-savvy and conventional café patrons
